Disks' hisotry is neccessary for us to ackownledge.It is also incredibly fast and interesting.In 1953 IBM recognized the immediate application for what it termed a "Random Access File" having high capacity and rapid random access at a relatively low cost.The IBM 1301 Disk Storage Unit, announced in 1961, introduced the usage of heads having self-acting air bearings with one head per each surface of the disks.Also in 1961, Bryant Computer Products introduced its 4000 series disk drives.Memorex in 1968 shipped the first HDD, the Memorex 630, plug compatible to an IBM model 2311 marking the beginning of independent competition (Plug Compatible Manufacturers or PCMs).In 1973, IBM introduced the IBM 3340 "Winchester" disk drive, the first significant commercial use of low mass and low load heads with lubricated platters.As the 1980s began, hard disk drives were a rare and very expensive optional feature on personal computers; however by the late '80s, hard disk drives were standard on all but the cheapest PC.
